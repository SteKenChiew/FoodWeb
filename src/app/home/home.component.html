What is Sunway Nom²?

Sunway Nom² is a go-to website for students at Sunway University to conveniently order food for pick-up and dine-in. We've carefully curated all your favourite dishes, catering to every taste bud and craving. From hearty breakfast options like Hashbrown Sandwich from Bite & Taste to satisfying lunches with flavoursome Aglio Olio from Daun Daun, from a quick brunch pick-me-up with a cup of Kopi to delightful teatime featuring Waffles and Kaya Ball from Tomyam Kitchen - we've got it all!

Don't waste time waiting for your food. Experience the fastest way to enjoy your meals on campus with Sunway Nom², your ultimate dine-in and pick-up service designed exclusively for Sunway University students.
